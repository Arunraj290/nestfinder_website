📅 Duration & Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement by providing a minimum of 1 calendar month's written notice after the minimum stay period.

In case of early vacating without proper notice, the tenant shall forfeit 1 month's rent from the security deposit.

✅ Tenant Responsibilities

  • Maintain the property in clean and good condition throughout the tenancy.
  • Pay electricity, water, internet, and other utility bills promptly.
  • Not sublet or assign any part of the premises to any third party.
  • Not carry out structural alterations without prior written consent from the landlord.
  • Inform the landlord of any damage or required repairs within 48 hours of occurrence.
  • Vacate the property on or before the agreed vacating date and return all keys.
  • Comply with all society rules, regulations, and maintenance norms.

🔐 Security Deposit Terms

The security deposit shall be refunded within 30 days after the tenant vacates the premises, subject to the following deductions:

  • Cost of any damage to the property beyond normal wear and tear.
  • Unpaid rent or utility dues.
  • Cost of professional cleaning if not maintained in original condition.
The security deposit shall not earn any interest during the tenancy period.

🚫 Prohibited Activities

  • No commercial activities in a residential property (unless permitted).
  • No subletting or paying guests without landlord's written consent.
  • No pets (unless agreed separately in writing).
  • No illegal activities on the premises.
  • No excessive noise between 10 PM and 7 AM.

🔧 Maintenance & Repairs

Landlord's responsibility: Major structural repairs, plumbing, electrical wiring, and appliances provided at the time of agreement.

Tenant's responsibility: Day-to-day maintenance, minor repairs below ₹1,000, and upkeep of appliances.

⚖️ Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising under this agreement shall be resolved through mutual discussion. If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, in the jurisdiction of the city where the property is located.
