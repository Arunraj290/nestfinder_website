NestFinder
NestFinder is a map-based rental discovery platform built for renters and property owners across India.
Instead of browsing endless lists, users can simply draw a circle on a map and instantly see all available rental properties within that area — no middlemen, no brokerage fees.

What This Website Does
For Renters:
Users open the map, search for any city or area, and draw a circle to define how far they are willing to travel. The map instantly shows all available houses, apartments, and shops within that circle. They can click any property to see photos, rent price, amenities, and owner contact details. They can also schedule a visit and view the rental agreement — all from the website.
For Property Owners:
Owners can upload their property listing by filling a simple form with photos, rent price, location, and rental terms. The listing goes live on the map for thousands of renters to discover.

Pages in This Website

Home — Introduction to NestFinder with a search bar and featured properties
Map Search — Interactive map where users draw a circle to find nearby rentals
Property Details — Full details of a property including photos, amenities, and owner info
Schedule Visit — A form to book a property visit with the owner
Rental Agreement — A preview of the rental terms and agreement
List Property — A form for property owners to upload their rental listing
